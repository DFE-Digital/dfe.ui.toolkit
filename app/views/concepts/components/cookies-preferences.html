Cookies on Department for Education

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

We use cookies to store information about how you use the DfE Sign-in website, such as the pages you visit.

Cookie settings

You can choose which cookies you're happy for us to use.

Cookies that measure website use

We use analytical software to measure how you use this website so we can improve it based on user needs. We do not allow Google or other third party analytical software providers to use or share data about how you use this site.

Cookies that remember your settings

These cookies do things like remember your preferences, the notifications you've seen, and the choices you make, to personalise your experience of using this site.

Strictly necessary cookies

These essential cookies do things like remember your progress through a form (for example a census).

They always need to be on.

Cookie settings

We use Javascript to set most of our cookies. Unfortunately Javascript is not running on your browser, so you cannot change your settings. You can try:

  • reloading the page
  • turning on Javascript in your browser
